# 安装

yarn add vuepress-theme-material || npm i vuepress-theme-material

docs/README.md

---
layout: 'home'
title: 'Home'
date: '2018-05-01'
---

layout home对应主页布局

假定你的文章都放在articles目录

docs/articles/*.md

需要给出以下基本信息

---
title: vuepress-theme-material
date: '2018-05-01 14:05:05'
tags:
    - vuepress
---
